Health Care Systems

In this project, I delved into the realm of healthcare systems and costs, focusing on both government and personal expenses. By utilizing data from the World Development Indicators (WDI) and the Nuclear Threat Initiative (NTI). Through rigorous data extraction, statistical analysis, and visualization techniques, I uncovered intriguing patterns and disparities. The project highlighted variations in government spending on healthcare across countries, while also exploring the implications of personal expenditure on individuals. This data-driven exploration deepened my knowledge of healthcare financing and demonstrated the power of insights to shape policy decisions in the ever-evolving healthcare landscape.
